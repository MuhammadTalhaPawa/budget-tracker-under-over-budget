The Math

How the calculation works

Simple and transparent. Budget divided evenly across 31 days, compared against actual spending.

Step 1
Daily = Total ÷ 31
Divide your monthly budget by 31 to get a fair daily allowance.
Step 2
Allowed = Daily × Day
Multiply daily budget by the current day to get your cumulative allowance.
Step 3
Diff = Allowed − Spent
Positive = under budget. Negative = over budget. Simple.

Smart Money Habits

Tips to stay on track

Simple practices that help you build and maintain a healthy budget all month long.

📋

Track every purchase

Log spending daily. Small expenses add up fast — a coffee here and a snack there can quietly blow your monthly budget.

📈

Check your status mid-month

Day 15 is the ideal checkpoint. Over budget halfway through? You still have time to cut back before the month ends.

🎯

Use the 50 / 30 / 20 rule

50% of income to needs, 30% to wants, 20% to savings. Run this tracker per category for deeper insight.

🚨

Set a personal buffer

Enter 90% of your real budget as the total. That hidden 10% acts as a cushion for unexpected costs at month’s end.
